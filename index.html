Welcome to PPCC

The University Of Cape Coast has a Chaplaincy Committee under the Academic Board responsible for the coordination of all religious activities in the University. Under the Chaplaincy Committee are the Four Chaplaincy Committees; Catholic, Anglican, Muslim and Protestant and Pentecostal Chaplaincy. It is the largest of the four chaplaincies under the Chaplaincy committee of the University Of Cape Coast. It was established in 1969 with a Chairman/Chaplain as a Head. It is responsible for the coordination of the activities of all the Protestant and Pentecostal groups in the University. The PPCC is made up of 23 Denominational and Non-Denominational Groups. PPCC has a Council as the highest decision making body which is made up of all Senior Members of the Group under PPCC, Principal Officers (Patrons), Executives Of the Groups ( President & Secretary) and PPCC executives committee members.
